Firefolio

Online Portfolio System

Download

Are you a programmer seeking a job in the games industry?

Firefolio has you covered. It's a self-hosted web application that lets you create and manage your very own portfolio website. Edit some files, upload them to your server and fill in the forms provided. That's it. You'll be surprised no-one told you about it before.

No fuss, just projects.

Too many porfolios are packed with information that your employer just simply isn't interested in. Firefolio fixes this by staying focused on what they really care about; your work.

Always up to date.

Powerful content management tools allow you to quickly add, remove and edit projects as you finish them.

Ready for anything.

Thanks to a comprehensive, real-time search engine, employers can find what they're looking for in seconds.

Say goodbye to tweaking your content for everyone you meet.
